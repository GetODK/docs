ODK Briefcase
================

:dfn:`ODK Briefcase` is a desktop application for pulling, pushing, and converting
form data on ODK servers such as :doc:`aggregate-intro`.
You can use it to move form definitions and records between different ODK servers,
archive the form data locally, or export it into useful formats.
